# Product Guidelines

## Tone and Style
Informative and accessible, catering to users with varying levels of expertise.

## Data Presentation
A strong emphasis on visualisations like charts and graphs, combined with tables and narrative summaries.

## Visual Identity
Use the visual identity defined in the grafisk-profil/ folder. The design should be clean, professional, and align with Skill's corporate branding.

## Methodology and Data Sources
Detailed explanations and links to source documentation.
