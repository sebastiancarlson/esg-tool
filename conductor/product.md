# Initial Concept

The ESG Tool is for CO2 calculations (Scope 1-3) and CSRD reporting. It uses Streamlit and Python.

# Product Definition

## Main Goals
The ESG Tool is designed to empower organizations to understand, manage, and report their environmental impact. Its primary goals are:

-   **Calculate CO2 emissions (Scope 1-3):** Provide accurate and comprehensive calculations of greenhouse gas emissions across all relevant scopes, enabling businesses to identify their main emission sources.
-   **Facilitate CSRD reporting:** Streamline the process of compiling and generating reports compliant with the Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive (CSRD), ensuring transparency and accountability.
-   **Provide a user-friendly interface for environmental data:** Offer an intuitive platform for users to input, visualize, and analyze environmental data, making it accessible to a wide range of stakeholders.

## Target Users
The ESG Tool aims to serve a diverse range of users within organizations:

-   **Sustainability managers:** Equip sustainability professionals with the tools they need to track progress, identify improvement opportunities, and drive sustainability initiatives.
-   **Financial analysts:** Enable financial professionals to integrate environmental data into financial analysis, risk assessment, and investment decisions.
-   **Corporate executives:** Provide executive leadership with a clear overview of the company's environmental performance, supporting strategic decision-making and stakeholder communication.

## Key Features

-   **Comprehensive Emissions Calculation:** Covering all three scopes of emissions, with detailed breakdowns by source and activity.
-   **CSRD Reporting Module:** Automating the generation of CSRD-compliant reports, including key performance indicators and narrative disclosures.
-   **Data Visualization Tools:** Interactive charts and graphs to explore emissions data and identify trends.
-   **PDF Export:** Ability to generate and export a PDF summary of the CSRD report for easy sharing and presentation.
-   **Benchmarking:** Compare environmental performance against industry peers and best practices.
-   **Integration Capabilities:** Seamless integration with existing data sources and systems for efficient data management.
